OPTICALCOHERRENCETOMOGRAPHY

Overview

Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) is a non-invasive imaging technique that plays a crucial role in medical diagnostics, particularly in ophthalmology. This technology utilizes the principles of low-coherence interferometry to generate high-resolution, cross-sectional images of biological tissues. OCT employs the interference of light waves to measure the echo time delay of reflections from different tissue layers. By analyzing these time delays, OCT produces detailed, real-time images of tissue microstructures.
